The Pace Hackathon

P.A College of Engineering, Dept of CSE organizes 2 days Hackathon under GLUG Pace on 20th and 21st of may 2023. Hackathon will be conducted at PACE campus.

Rules:

  • Participation: Students can participate either as individuals or in teams.
  • Team Size: Teams are limited to a maximum of four members.
  • Allotted Space: Each team will be provided with a designated workspace. If a team requires any additional assistance or resources, they must inform the organizers in advance.
  • Original Work: All projects submitted must be original and developed during the hackathon. Plagiarism or the use of pre-existing solutions will result in disqualification.
  • Equipment and Materials: Participants are responsible for bringing their own laptops, devices, and any necessary software or tools. The organizers will provide basic amenities such as power outlets and Wi-Fi.
  • Intellectual Property: Participants retain ownership of the intellectual property rights associated with their projects. However, by participating in the hackathon, participants grant the organizers a non-exclusive license to showcase and promote their projects.
  • Judging Criteria: Projects will be evaluated based on criteria such as innovation, technical complexity, usability, and impact. The judging panel's decision is final.
  • Winners Announcement: The winners will be announced on the second day of the hackathon, following the completion of the judging process.
  • Compliance with Laws: Participants must comply with all applicable laws and regulations during the hackathon.
  • Safety and Security: Participants are responsible for their own personal belongings and should take necessary precautions to ensure their safety and the security of their belongings.
